The Information About BMW

BMW (or Bayerische Motoren Werke in German , lit. "Bavarian Engine Manufactory"), is a German manufacturer of high-end, sports and luxury automobiles and motorcycles , after having been a major manufacturer of aircraft engines . The company was founded in 1916 by Gustav Otto and Karl Friedrich Rapp . The BMW brand is part of the BMW Group along with Mini and Rolls-Royce . BMW's headquarters and museum are located in Munich , Germany . In 2018, BMW sold 2,125,026 motor vehicles and 123,495 two-wheelers ( BMW Motorrad ).

History Of BMW
BMW is created onMarch 7 , 19162 , by merger of two Munich mechanical firms , the Bayerische Flugzeugwerke and Rapp Motorenwerke , a manufacturer of aircraft engines. The BMW brand emblem takes up the black circle of Rapp's logo, with blue and white in the center symbolizing the colors of Bavaria . When the company became the " Bayerische Motoren Werke " in 1917, its leaders decided to use the colors of Bavaria. However, to spare the susceptibilities of the very centralizing monarchy at the time ( Germany has only been united since 1871) and avoid reviving regionalist tendencies, the Bavarian colors are reversed, blue passing before white. In 1929, twelve years after the creation of the BMW logo, a link was made on an advertisement with the symbolization of a rotating propeller (the white quarters represent this propeller, the blue the sky) 3 .
